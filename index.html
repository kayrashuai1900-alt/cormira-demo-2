AI 行为科学AI for Behavioral Science

让 AI 照见人心Let AI understand human behavior

Cormira（扶光心舍）是一家专注于 AI 行为科学的研究与产品公司。我们研究如何用结构化模型理解、预测和解释人类行为，并将这些方法应用于心理健康及真实世界中的行为研究。Cormira is a research and product company focused on AI for behavioral science. We study how structured models can understand, predict, and explain human behavior, and apply these methods to mental health and real-world behavioral research.

构建行为模型Model behavior

我们不将人类行为简化为情绪打分或关键词匹配，而是研究情境、动机、约束与决策之间的关系。We don't reduce human behavior to sentiment scores or keyword matching. We study the relationships between context, motivation, constraints, and decisions.

严谨因果推断Rigorous causal inference

我们区分相关与因果，用实验与因果方法评估哪些因素真正影响结果。We distinguish correlation from causation, using experiments and causal methods to evaluate what truly affects outcomes.

提高模型可解释性Improve interpretability

模型输出应能被人类审查、追溯与修正。高风险场景不接受无法解释的黑箱结论。Model outputs should be reviewable, traceable, and correctable. High-stakes settings should not rely on unexplained black-box conclusions.

这三项，是我们所有工作的共同基础。These three principles are the foundation of everything we do.

高保真人类行为模拟High-fidelity behavior simulation

我们基于自研算法，构建可控制、可复现的人类行为模拟环境。在企业端，已用于消费者决策研究、产品概念测试与营销策略预演；在高校与研究机构，可替代部分真人被试实验，降低成本并提升可复现性；在医疗场景，正探索患者行为预测与干预方案的预评估。Using our own algorithms, we build controllable, reproducible environments for simulating human behavior. In enterprise settings, deployed for consumer decision research, concept testing, and marketing strategy rehearsal; in universities and research institutions, partially replacing human participant studies to reduce cost and improve reproducibility; in healthcare, exploring patient behavior prediction and intervention pre-assessment.

我们的长期方向Our long-term direction 持续研究中Ongoing research

从行为模拟，到 Agent 治理From behavior simulation to agent governance

模拟人类行为只是起点。AI 正越来越多地参与判断与决策。我们研究如何评估这些系统的行为、解释其决策依据，并建立能够被人类审查和约束的评测框架。Simulating human behavior is only the beginning. As AI increasingly participates in judgment and decision-making, we study how to evaluate its behavior, explain its reasoning, and build frameworks that people can review and constrain.
